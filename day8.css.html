About : Fly - Feel The High

Fly - Feel The High is a trendy lounge located in Jubilee Hills area of Hyderabad, offering an array of bar food, beverages, Chinese, Continental, Italian, North Indian, and pizza. The lounge is known for its vibrant atmosphere with live music entertainment by a DJ and a live band playing Bollywood songs. The lounge has an outdoor seating area and a rooftop with stunning city views, along with a designated smoking area. Guests can dress casually or semi-formal, creating a comfortable yet stylish ambiance.

Fly - Feel The High is a great spot to hang out with friends or celebrate special occasions, with happy hours offering discounted drinks during select hours. The legal drinking age is 21 plus. Overall, Fly - Feel The High provides a unique and exciting experience with facilities such as a full bar, live DJ, and live music.
