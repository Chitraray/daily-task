JAWAN MOVIE JAWAN 
  
 
   
CAST : Shahrukh khan,Nayanthara,Vijaysethupath,Sanya malhotra,Priyamani,Deepika padukone,Girija oak,Lehar khan,Sunil grover,Ridhi dogra

     Director : Atlee Kumar
     Producer : Gauri Khan
     Writers :  Sumit Arora , Atlee , Ramanagirivasan


DESCRIPTION : A man is driven by a personal vendetta to rectify the wrongs in society, while keeping a promise made years ago.
                 He comes up against a monstrous outlaw with no fear, who has caused extreme suffering to many.
                 A high-octane action thriller which outlines the emotional journey of a man who is set to rectify the wrongs in the society.
